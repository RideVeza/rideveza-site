Is your Play Store region set to Canada?

Your phone must be set to Canada. If it is not, the Veza Mobile App will not show in the Play Store. The exact names, buttons, and screens may look a little different on your phone — look for the closest match.

  1. Open the Play Store

    Open the Google Play Store app.

  2. Tap your profile picture

    Tap your profile picture in the top-right corner.

  3. Tap Settings

    In the menu that opens, scroll down and tap Settings.

  4. Tap General

    Tap General.

  5. Tap Account and device preferences

    Tap Account and device preferences.

  6. Check your country

    Under Country and profiles, make sure Canada is selected.

    If it is not, and you cannot change it, please speak to your administrator. Google only lets you change country once a year, and it needs a Canadian payment method. You can also read Google’s guide to changing your Play country.
