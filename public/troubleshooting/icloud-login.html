Are you logged in to iCloud?

Your passkey is saved to your iCloud Keychain, so your iPhone has to be signed in to an Apple Account before you can create one.

  1. Open Settings

    Open the Settings app (the grey gear icon). If it's not on your Home Screen, swipe down on the middle of the screen and type "Settings" to find it.

  2. Tap your name

    Your name sits at the very top of the Settings list. Tap it to open your Apple Account (older phones call this your Apple ID).

  3. Check that you're signed in

    If you see your name and email, you're signed in. If it says "Sign in to your iPhone", tap it and sign in with your Apple Account.

  4. Confirm iCloud is on

    Tap "iCloud" and make sure it shows as turned on.
