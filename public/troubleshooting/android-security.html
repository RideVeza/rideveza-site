Do you have security on your phone?

You open a passkey with your fingerprint, face, or screen lock. You need a screen lock set.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your phone. If you cannot find it, swipe up to see all your apps and look for “Settings”.

  2. Open Security

    Tap Security. On some phones it is called Security & privacy or Lock screen. If you cannot find it, use the search bar at the top and type Screen lock.

  3. Check your screen lock

    Under Screen lock, check that a PIN, pattern, or password is set. If it says None or Swipe, tap it and set one.
