Does your phone have enough free space?

The Veza Mobile App needs at least 250 MB of free space. If your phone is full, it cannot download.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your phone. If you cannot find it, swipe up to see all your apps and look for “Settings”.

  2. Open “Storage”

    Tap “Storage”. On some phones it is inside “Battery and device care” or “Device care” first.

  3. Check the free space

    The screen shows the space used and the space that is free. You need at least 250 MB free.

    If it is almost full, delete apps, photos, or videos you do not need. Then download the Veza Mobile App again.
