Is your App Store region set to Canada?

Your iPhone must be set to Canada. If it is not, the Veza Mobile App will not show in the App Store.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  2. Tap your name

    If you are signed in correctly, your name is shown at the top of the Settings app. Tap it. If you do not see your name, you are not signed in — sign in first.

  3. Tap “Media & Purchases”

    Scroll down and tap “Media & Purchases”. A menu opens. Tap “View Account”. Enter your password if asked.

  4. Check “Country/Region”

    Find “Country/Region”. It should say Canada. If it does, you are done.

i

If it does not say Canada, tap “Country/Region”, then “Change Country or Region”, and choose Canada. Apple may ask you to spend any store credit and add a Canadian payment method first. If you get stuck, please speak to your administrator.
