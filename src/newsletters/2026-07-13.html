Week of July 13, 2026

Last week at Veza

Veza Web picked up several payroll and planning upgrades this week, plus staff can now request time off from the mobile app, and Veza Guardian got a privacy update. Here is what your team will notice.
 

Veza Web

Set pay rates by operational period

Pay rates can now be set for each operational period, so the right rates apply to the right school year. You can also tag pay with payroll codes, which keeps everything sorted when it is time to run payroll.

How to find it

Payroll → Payrates → use the period dropdown above the table to see each period's rates (a rate's period is set when it is first created). Payroll codes appear on pay in Paylog.

Quickly find runs that are missing pay

A new filter shows runs where a role has no pay set — driver, attendant, LPN, or supervisor. It is an easy way to catch anything that would otherwise be missed before payroll.

How to find it

Dispatch → open the filters → Roles without pay → choose the roles to check.

Paylog loads much faster

We reworked how the Paylog page loads, so it now opens in a fraction of the time it used to. Reviewing pay is quicker, even on busy days with a lot of runs.

More at a glance on Routes and Assignments

The Routes table now shows how many riders are on each route. And on the Assignments view, you can filter by supervisor to focus on one person's routes at a time.

How to find it

Routes → the Student Count column on the table. For the supervisor filter: Routes → Assignments → Select supervisor.

More vehicle details on the Units table

The Units table now shows more about each vehicle at a glance — CVIP number, fuel type, weight, tire size, and maintenance dates like the last oil change and last tire change. It is easier to keep an eye on inspections and upkeep across your fleet.

How to find it

Units → the new columns appear on the table; use the column menu to show or hide any.
 

Veza Mobile

Staff can request time off from the app

Bus staff can now submit time off requests right from the mobile app. Your office team reviews and manages every request in Veza Web — approving or declining without paper forms or phone calls.

How to find it

In the mobile app, staff submit a time off request. Manage them in Veza Web: Staff → Time off requests.

A complete support site for the mobile app

Every screen in the mobile app now has a plain-language guide — signing in, runs, forms, expenses, time off, and more. It is a simple place to send anyone on your team who needs a hand getting started.

How to find it

Go to rideveza.com/support/mobile
 

Veza Guardian

Staff last names are now hidden from parents

To protect your staff's privacy, the Guardian app no longer shows their last names. Parents still see who is on the bus by first name, without sharing more than they need to.
