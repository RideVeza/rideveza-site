Is your Play Store region set to Canada?

Your phone must be set to Canada. If it is not, the Veza app will not show in the Play Store.

  1. Open the Play Store

    Open the Google Play Store app.

  2. Open your profile

    Tap your profile picture in the top-right corner. Then tap Settings.

  3. Check your country

    Tap General, then Account and device preferences. Check that the country shows Canada.

    If it does not, and you cannot change it, please speak to your administrator. Google only lets you change country once a year, and it needs a Canadian payment method.
