Does your iPhone support passkeys?

A passkey needs iOS 16 or newer. This means an iPhone 8 or newer. The steps below show how to check your iPhone.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  2. Tap “General”

    In the Settings app, tap “General”.

  3. Tap “About”

    At the top of the list, tap “About”.

  4. Check your iOS version

    Look at “iOS Version”. It must be 16 or higher, on an iPhone 8 or newer.

    If it is below 16, update it. Go to General › Software Update. If there is no update, your iPhone is too old. Please speak to your administrator.
