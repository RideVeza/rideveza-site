Is your Play Store region set to Canada?

The Veza app is published in the Canadian Play Store. You need to install it from there before you can set up your passkey.

  1. Open the Play Store

    Open the Google Play Store app.

  2. Open your profile

    Tap your profile icon in the top-right corner, then tap "Settings".

  3. Check your country

    Tap "General", then "Account and device preferences", and confirm the country shows Canada. If it doesn't show Canada and you can't change it, contact your administrator — Google only lets you switch Play Store countries once a year, and it needs a payment method with a Canadian address.
