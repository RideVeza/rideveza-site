Are you logged in to your Gmail account?

Your passkey is saved to your Google account through Google Password Manager, so you have to be signed in to Gmail before you can create one.

  1. Open Settings

    Open the Settings app (the gear icon). It's in your app drawer — or swipe down from the top of the screen and tap the gear icon.

  2. Open Accounts

    Tap "Accounts" (on some phones it's called "Passwords & accounts"). Can't find it? Use the search bar at the top of Settings and type "Accounts".

  3. Check for your Google account

    Confirm your Google (Gmail) account is listed. If it isn't, tap "Add account", choose "Google", and sign in.
