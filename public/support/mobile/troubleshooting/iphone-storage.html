Does your iPhone have enough free space?

The Veza Mobile App needs at least 250 MB of free space. If your iPhone is full, it cannot download.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  2. Tap “General”

    In the Settings app, tap “General”.

  3. Tap “About”

    At the top of the list, tap “About”.

  4. Look at “Available”

    Scroll down to “Available”. This is your free space. You need at least 250 MB free.

    If it is low, delete apps, photos, or videos you do not need. Then download the Veza Mobile App again.
