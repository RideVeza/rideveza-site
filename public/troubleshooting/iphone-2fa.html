Is two-factor authentication turned on?

Two-factor authentication keeps your Apple Account safe. It must be turned on to make a passkey.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  2. Tap your name

    If you are signed in correctly, your name is shown at the top of the Settings app. Tap it. If you do not see your name, you are not signed in — sign in first.

  3. Tap “Sign-In & Security”

    On the next screen, tap “Sign-In & Security”.

  4. Check “Two-Factor Authentication”

    Under Security, tap “Two-Factor Authentication”. The next screen tells you if it is on. If it is on, you are done.

    If it is off, follow the steps to turn it on and add a phone number. If you get stuck, please speak to your administrator.
