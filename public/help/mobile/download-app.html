Downloading the app

The Veza Mobile App is in the Canadian app store. Your phone must be set to Canada and have enough free space. Before contacting your administrator, ensure you’ve checked each of the topics below.

iPhone Apple iOS devices 3 checks ›

  • Are you signed in to your Apple Account?

    You must be signed in to your Apple Account to download apps. If you are not signed in, the app will not install.

    Show me how →

  • Is your App Store region set to Canada?

    Your iPhone must be set to Canada. If it is not, the Veza Mobile App will not show in the App Store.

    Show me how →

  • Does your iPhone have enough free space?

    Your iPhone needs at least 250 MB of free space to download the app.

    Show me how →

When your iPhone is ready, get the Veza Mobile App from the App Store.

    You need to be signed in to the Play Store with your Google Account to download the app. This is the account you use for Gmail.
