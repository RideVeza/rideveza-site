Do you have security on your phone?

You open a passkey with your fingerprint, face, or screen lock. You need a screen lock set. The exact names, buttons, and screens may look a little different on your phone — look for the closest match.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your phone. If you cannot find it, swipe up to see all your apps and look for “Settings”.

  2. Open “Security & privacy”

    Tap Security & privacy. On a Samsung phone, tap Lock screen instead. If you cannot find it, use the search bar at the top and type Screen lock.

  3. Open “Device unlock”

    Tap Device unlock. On some phones this step is not needed, and you will see Screen lock right away.

  4. Check your screen lock

    Look at Screen lock. It must be set to a PIN, pattern, or password. If it says None or Swipe, tap it and set one.
