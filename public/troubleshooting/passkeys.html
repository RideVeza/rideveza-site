Setting up your passkey

A passkey lets you sign in to the Veza app securely, without a password. Before you can create one, your phone needs a few things in place. Find your device below and run through the checks — they clear up the most common passkey problems. If you’re still stuck afterward, contact your administrator.

iPhone Apple iOS devices 4 checks ›

  • Are you logged in to iCloud?

    Your passkey is saved to your iCloud Keychain, so your iPhone has to be signed in to an Apple Account before you can create one.

    Show me how →

  • Do you have the Passwords app on your phone?

    The Passwords app (iOS 18 and later) is where you find and manage your passkey. If it’s not on your phone you may have deleted it — you can re-download it from the App Store, or update iOS if your phone doesn’t have it yet.

    Show me how →

  • Do you have security on your phone?

    A passkey is unlocked with Face ID, Touch ID, or your passcode — so your iPhone needs one of those turned on before you can create a passkey.

    Show me how →

  • Is your App Store region set to Canada?

    The Veza app is published in the Canadian App Store. You need to install it from there before you can set up your passkey.

    Show me how →
