Do you have security on your phone?

You open a passkey with Face ID or a passcode. You need one of these — not both.

i

Older iPhones use “Touch ID & Passcode” instead of Face ID. The steps are the same — turn on Touch ID or a passcode.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  2. Tap “Face ID & Passcode”

    Scroll down and tap “Face ID & Passcode”. Enter your passcode if asked.

  3. Turn on Face ID or a passcode

    You only need one. Pick the one that is easier for you:

    Face ID: tap “Set Up Face ID” and follow the steps.

    Passcode: tap “Turn Passcode On” and choose a passcode you can remember.
