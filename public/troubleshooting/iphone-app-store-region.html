Is your App Store region set to Canada?

The Veza app is published in the Canadian App Store. You need to install it from there before you can set up your passkey.

  1. Open Settings and tap your name

    Open the Settings app (the grey gear icon) — if it's not on your Home Screen, swipe down on the middle of the screen and search for "Settings". Then tap your name at the top.

  2. Open Media & Purchases

    Tap "Media & Purchases", then "View Account". Sign in if you're asked to.

  3. Check Country/Region

    Tap "Country/Region" and confirm it shows Canada. If it doesn't, tap "Change Country or Region" and choose Canada. If you can't switch — Apple asks you to use up any store credit and add a Canadian payment method first — contact your administrator.
