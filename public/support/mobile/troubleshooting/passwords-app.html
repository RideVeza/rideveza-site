Do you have the Passwords app, and is it syncing with iCloud?

Your passkey is kept in the Passwords app. The app must be on your phone, and it must save to iCloud.

  1. Confirm you have the Passwords app

    The Passwords app may be on your Home Screen, in a folder, or in your App Library. If you cannot find it, you can download it for free from the App Store.

  2. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  3. Tap your name

    If you are signed in correctly, your name is shown at the top of the Settings app. Tap it. If you do not see your name, you are not signed in — sign in first.

  4. Tap “iCloud”

    On the next screen, tap “iCloud”.

  5. Tap “See All”

    Find “Saved to iCloud”. Tap “See All”.

  6. Tap “Passwords & Keychain”

    Find “Passwords & Keychain” in the list. Tap it.

  7. Turn on “Sync this iPhone”

    The switch must be green. If it is grey, tap it to turn it on.
