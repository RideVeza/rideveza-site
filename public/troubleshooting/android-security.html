Do you have security on your phone?

A passkey is unlocked with your fingerprint, face, or screen lock — so your phone needs a screen lock set before you can create a passkey.

  1. Open Settings

    Open the Settings app (the gear icon). It's in your app drawer — or swipe down from the top of the screen and tap the gear icon.

  2. Open Security

    Tap "Security" (on some phones it's called "Security & privacy" or "Lock screen"). Can't find it? Use the search bar at the top of Settings and type "Screen lock".

  3. Check your screen lock

    Under "Screen lock", make sure a PIN, pattern, or password is set. If it says "None" or "Swipe", tap it and set one up.
