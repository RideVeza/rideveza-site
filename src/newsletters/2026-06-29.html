What's new at Veza
Week of June 29, 2026

Last week at Veza
Last week was a busy week for route planning in Veza Web, plus updates for the driver and Guardian apps. Here is what your team will notice.
 

Veza Web

Filter routes by school year

Plan next year without mixing it into this year. The Routes page can now be filtered by school year.
How to find it

Routes → pick the school year from the dropdown at the top of the page.

Email a route PDF

Share a route without leaving Veza. Choose your recipients and Veza emails them a print-ready PDF of the route's details.
How to find it

Routes → open a route's details → Email PDF → select recipients.

Permanent or temporary route assignments

Assign staff to a route for good, or just for now. Temporary assignments are tracked with their own status, and a new toggle lets you choose whether the unit moves with the driver.
How to find it

Routes → assign staff to a route → choose Permanent or Temporary.

See support and equipment needs on the Routes table

New columns show which routes need an Attendant or LPN, and what equipment each route requires — so you always know what people and equipment a route needs.

New Assignments view on the Routes page

See every route's assignments together in one grid — each assignee, their status, their contact information, and their pay rates.
How to find it

Routes → switch to Assignments at the top of the page.

Assigned routes on the Staff page

Each staff member's assigned route now shows directly on the Staff table — so you can see your staff's assignments and easily identify staff with no routes.
 

Veza Mobile

Troubleshooting guide published

A step-by-step guide for the most common driver app issues — downloading the app, creating a passkey, and signing in. Use the link below to share with those that need it.
How to find it

Go to rideveza.com/support/mobile
 

Veza Guardian

Veza Guardian is now on Google Play

The Guardian app for parents is now published on Google Play, with the iPhone version in review with Apple. Parents will be able to see their children's routes and follow the bus with live status updates. Please note: the app is still in testing — it is visible in the store, but not yet ready for families to use.
How to find it

Go to Veza Guardian on Google Play
