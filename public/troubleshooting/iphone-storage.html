Does your iPhone have enough free space?

If your iPhone is full, the Veza app cannot download.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  2. Tap “General”

    In the Settings app, tap “General”.

  3. Tap “iPhone Storage”

    Tap “iPhone Storage”. Wait a moment while your iPhone checks the space.

  4. Check the space at the top

    The bar at the top shows the space used. The number shows the space that is free. You need at least 1 GB free.

    If it is almost full, delete apps, photos, or videos you do not need. Then download the Veza app again.
