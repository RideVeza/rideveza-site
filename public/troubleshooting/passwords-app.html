Do you have the Passwords app on your phone?

The Passwords app (iOS 18 and later) is where you find and manage your passkey. If it's not on your phone you may have deleted it — you can re-download it from the App Store, or update iOS if your phone doesn't have it yet.

  1. Search for it

    Swipe down on your home screen to open search, then type "Passwords".

  2. Open the app

    If the Passwords app appears in the results, tap it to open — you're all set.

  3. If it's missing, re-download it

    The Passwords app can be deleted by accident. Open the App Store, search for "Passwords" by Apple, and tap the download icon to get it back.

  4. Still can't find it? Update iOS

    If the Passwords app doesn't show up in the App Store either, open Settings, tap "General", then "Software Update", and install any update. The app needs iOS 18 or later.
