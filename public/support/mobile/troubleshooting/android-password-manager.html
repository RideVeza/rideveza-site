Is a password manager turned on?

Your passkey is saved by a password manager. On most phones this is Google Password Manager. On Samsung phones it may be Samsung Pass. One must be turned on, or your passkey cannot be saved. The exact names, buttons, and screens may look a little different on your phone — look for the closest match.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your phone. If you cannot find it, swipe up to see all your apps and look for “Settings”.

  2. Open “Passwords, passkeys & accounts”

    Tap Passwords, passkeys & accounts. On a Samsung phone, tap General management, then Passwords, passkeys, and autofill. If you cannot find it, use the search bar at the top and type Passwords.

  3. Check your password manager

    At the top, look at Preferred service. It should show Google Password Manager. On a Samsung phone it may say Samsung Pass. If it shows None, tap Change and choose Google Password Manager.
