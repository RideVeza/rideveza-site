Week of July 6, 2026

Last week at Veza

A big week: the Veza Guardian app for parents is now on both app stores and ready to use. Drivers can see their route assignments in the mobile app, and staff records now stay in sync with BambooHR. Here is what your team will notice.
 

Veza Web

Staff records sync from BambooHR

If your organization uses BambooHR, your staff list in Veza now stays up to date on its own. Veza syncs with BambooHR four times a day, so new staff and updated details flow in automatically — no need to enter anyone twice.

How to find it

Staff → the BambooHR badge at the top shows when the last sync ran → select Sync to update right away.

More equipment types for units

New equipment types have been added to the available equipment on units, so you can record more of what each vehicle carries.

How to find it

Units → open a unit's details → Equipment and Capabilities tab.
 

Veza Mobile

Route assignments in the mobile app

Drivers can now see the routes they are assigned to, and which days they run, right in the mobile app. No need to wait for a printed schedule or a call from dispatch.

How to find it

Open the mobile app → Runs tab → your assigned routes are at the bottom, below your daily schedule.
 

Veza Guardian

Veza Guardian is on both app stores — and ready to use

The Guardian app for parents is now published on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, and it is ready for families. Parents can see their children's runs and follow the bus with live updates on the way to and from school.

How to find it

Search for “Veza Guardian” in the Apple App Store or the Google Play Store.

Bus location is hidden near a driver's home

When the bus is within 100 metres of the driver's home, its position is masked in the Guardian app. This adds privacy for drivers who park their bus at home — parents still see live updates for the rest of the run.
