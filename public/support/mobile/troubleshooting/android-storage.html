Does your phone have enough free space?

The Veza Mobile App needs at least 250 MB of free space. If your phone is full, it cannot download. The exact names, buttons, and screens may look a little different on your phone — look for the closest match.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your phone. If you cannot find it, swipe up to see all your apps and look for “Settings”.

  2. Open “Storage”

    Tap “Storage”. On a Samsung phone, tap “Battery and device care” first, then “Storage”.

  3. Check the free space

    The screen shows the space used and the space that is free. You need at least 250 MB free. If it is almost full, delete apps, photos, or videos you do not need. Then download the Veza Mobile App again.
