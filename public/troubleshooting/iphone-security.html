Do you have security on your phone?

A passkey is unlocked with Face ID, Touch ID, or your passcode — so your iPhone needs one of those turned on before you can create a passkey.

  1. Open Settings

    Open the Settings app (the grey gear icon). If it's not on your Home Screen, swipe down on the middle of the screen and type "Settings" to find it.

  2. Open Face ID & Passcode

    Tap "Face ID & Passcode" (older phones show "Touch ID & Passcode"). Enter your passcode if asked.

  3. Make sure a passcode is on

    If you see "Turn Passcode On", tap it and set a passcode. If a passcode is already on, you're good.
