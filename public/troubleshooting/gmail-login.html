Are you logged in to your Gmail account?

Your passkey is saved to your Google account. You must be signed in to Gmail first.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your phone. If you cannot find it, swipe up to see all your apps and look for “Settings”.

  2. Open Accounts

    Tap Accounts. On some phones it is called Passwords & accounts. If you cannot find it, use the search bar at the top and type Accounts.

  3. Check for your Google account

    Check that your Gmail account is in the list. If it is not, tap Add account, choose Google, and sign in.
