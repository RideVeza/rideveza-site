Are you signed in to your Google Account?

You need to be signed in to your Google Account on your phone. This is the same account you use for Gmail. The exact names, buttons, and screens may look a little different on your phone — look for the closest match.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your phone. If you cannot find it, swipe up to see all your apps and look for “Settings”.

  2. Open “Passwords, passkeys & accounts”

    Tap Passwords, passkeys & accounts. On a Samsung phone, tap Accounts and backup, then Manage accounts. If you cannot find it, use the search bar at the top and type Accounts.

  3. Check for your Google account

    Check that your Google account is in the list. If it is there, you are signed in and you are done.

  4. If it is not there, add it

    Tap Add account, choose Google, and sign in with your email and password.
