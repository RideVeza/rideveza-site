Setting up your account

Your Veza account signs in with a passkey instead of a password. Get your phone ready to make a passkey, then set up your account from your invitation email.

Before getting started

Your phone needs a few things ready before it can make a passkey. Before contacting your administrator, ensure you’ve checked each of the topics for your device.

iPhone Apple iOS devices 5 checks ›

  • Does your iPhone support passkeys?

    You need an iPhone 8 or newer, with iOS 16 or later. Older iPhones cannot make a passkey.

    Show me how →

  • Are you signed in to your Apple Account?

    Your passkey is saved to your Apple Account. You must be signed in first.

    Show me how →

  • Is two-factor authentication turned on?

    Your Apple Account needs two-factor authentication turned on. Without it, you cannot make a passkey.

    Show me how →

  • Do you have the Passwords app, and is it syncing with iCloud?

    Your passkey is kept in the Passwords app. The app must be on your phone, and it must save to iCloud.

    Show me how →

  • Do you have security on your phone?

    You open a passkey with Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode. You need one of these turned on.

    Show me how →

You can also read Apple’s guide to passkeys.

Android Samsung, Google & other 4 checks ›

  • Does your phone support passkeys?

    You need Android 9 or newer. Most phones from 2018 onward have this. Older phones cannot make a passkey.

    Show me how →

  • Are you signed in to your Google Account?

    Your passkey is saved to your Google Account. You must be signed in first. This is the account you use for Gmail.

    Show me how →

  • Is a password manager turned on?

    Your passkey is saved by a password manager, like Google Password Manager or Samsung Pass. One must be turned on. If it was removed or turned off, your passkey cannot be saved.

    Show me how →

  • Do you have security on your phone?

    You open a passkey with your fingerprint, face, or screen lock. You need a screen lock set — a fingerprint or face is optional.

Creating your account

Once your phone is ready, creating your account takes just a couple of minutes.

  1. Get an invitation email

    Your administrator sends you an invitation email with a passkey setup link. It comes from Veza <donotreply@rideveza.com> and does not accept replies.

  2. Tap the link in your invitation

    After downloading the app, tap the link in your invitation email. It opens the app, and your phone guides you through setting up your passkey — this looks different on every device.

    Your invitation link expires 48 hours after it is sent — if it has expired, ask your administrator to send a new one.

  3. You’re signed in

    Once your passkey is set up, you are signed in to your account. From now on, use your passkey whenever you sign in.

Passkey stopped working, or been removed? Contact your administrator for a new invitation email and set up your account again.
