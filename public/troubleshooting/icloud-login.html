Are you signed in to your Apple Account?

You must be signed in to your Apple Account. You need this to download the Veza app, and to create your passkey.

  1. Open the Settings app

    Open the Settings app on your iPhone. If you cannot find it, swipe down on the middle of the Home Screen and search for “Settings”.

  2. Check that you are signed in

    Look at the top of the Settings app. The two pictures below show the difference.

    ✓ Signed in

    Your name is shown at the top.

    × Not signed in

    It says “Sign in to access your iCloud data”.

  3. If you are not signed in, sign in

    Tap “Apple Account” at the top of Settings. Then follow the steps to sign in. Your name will then show at the top.

    Tap “Apple Account” at the top of the Settings app.

    Choose how to sign in. Then follow the steps.

i

Do not have an Apple Account? You can make one for free. On the sign-in screen, tap “Don’t have an Apple Account?” and follow the steps. If you need help, please speak to your administrator.
