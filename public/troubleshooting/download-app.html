Downloading the app

The Veza app is in the Canadian app store. Your phone must be set to Canada and have enough free space. Find your phone below and check each one. If you are still stuck, please speak to your administrator.

iPhone Apple iOS devices 3 checks ›

  • Are you signed in to your Apple Account?

    You must be signed in to your Apple Account to download apps. If you are not signed in, the app will not install.

    Show me how →

  • Is your App Store region set to Canada?

    Your iPhone must be set to Canada. If it is not, the Veza app will not show in the App Store.

    Show me how →

  • Does your iPhone have enough free space?

    Your iPhone needs at least 250 MB of free space to download the app.
